Frequently asked

Questions clients ask before booking.

Do you write papers for clients?

No. I edit, coach, and give feedback on writing that's already yours. That means line editing, structural feedback, source guidance, and formatting help — not ghostwriting an assignment for you to submit as your own work. This keeps the work honest and keeps you in compliance with your school's academic integrity policy.

What citation styles do you work with?

APA (6th and 7th edition), MLA, and Chicago (notes-bibliography and author-date). Tell me your program's required style when you send your draft.

How long does editing take?

Most single documents — essays, personal statements, journal articles — are returned within 5 to 10 business days. Dissertation coaching runs on a longer, ongoing schedule set at intake. Rush turnaround may be available; ask when you send your project.

Do you work with clients outside the US and Canada?

My primary hours cover US and Canadian time zones (EST through PST), but I take on international clients when schedules line up. Ask if you're unsure.

Is my work kept confidential?

Yes. Drafts, personal statements, and research are not shared, published, or reused without your written permission.
